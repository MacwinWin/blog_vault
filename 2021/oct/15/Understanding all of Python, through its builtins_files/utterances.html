Correction at https://sadh.life/post/builtins/#type-the-class-factory

MyClass = type('MyClass', (MySuperClass), {'x': x_function})

should be

MyClass = type('MyClass', (MySuperClass,), {'x': x_function})

since the second param is a tuple and commas – not parentheses! – make tuples.
